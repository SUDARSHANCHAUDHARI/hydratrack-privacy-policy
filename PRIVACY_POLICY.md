# HydraTrack Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 21, 2026
_Last updated: March 21, 2026_
**Version:** 1.0.0

HydraTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is a hydration tracking app for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
- Daily water intake logs, hydration goals, and drink preferences are stored on your device using Room database.
- No account or sign-in is required.

### Google Fit (Optional)
- If you choose to sync with Google Fit, your hydration data is shared with Google Fit on your device.
- This sync is entirely optional and controlled by you.
- See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for Google Fit data practices.

## How We Use Your Information

- Track and display your daily water intake and hydration goals.
- Send hydration reminder notifications.
- Optionally sync with Google Fit when enabled by you.

We do not transmit your hydration data to any SudarshanTechLabs server. We do not sell or share your data.

## Storage and Retention

- All hydration data is stored exclusively on your device.
- Data is retained until you clear app data or uninstall the app.

## Data Security

- Local data is protected by Android's built-in storage encryption.

## Permissions Used

- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** (Android 13+): Required to send hydration reminders throughout the day.
- **ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION**: Used optionally to adjust hydration goals based on physical activity.
- **INTERNET**: Required only if Google Fit sync is enabled.

## Your Rights and Controls

- Delete all local data by clearing app data in Android Settings or uninstalling the app.
- Disable Google Fit sync at any time in app settings.

## Children's Privacy

HydraTrack is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
